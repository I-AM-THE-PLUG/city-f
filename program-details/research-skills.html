Advanced training for social researchers and consultants with comprehensive understanding of quantitative and qualitative methodologies.

Program Overview

This high-level programme is for those who wish to work as social researchers and consultants in a variety of settings, e.g. public sector, private sector, or operate as independent researchers. Graduates will also be well-prepared for further academic research.

The programme offers an advanced understanding of quantitative and qualitative research methodologies as well as the ethics of research. With the practical courses on the Skills of Professional Researchers, participants will be equipped with the necessary organizational skills to help them start in a diversity of work environments or further research.

The significant research project is an opportunity to apply the learning throughout the programme. This is a very advanced research skills program and only applicants with prior research experience or training at undergraduate or postgraduate level will be admitted.

Programme Curriculum

  • Researching in a Global World (epistemology; ontology; methods; techniques; cultural considerations)
  • Advanced Qualitative Research
  • Advanced Quantitative Research
  • Ethics in Social Research
  • Skills of Professional Researcher Workshops
  • Research Project (30,000 words)

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this programme are prepared for various roles including:

  • Research officers in the public or private sectors
  • Private consultants
  • Policy analysts
  • Market researchers
  • Academic researchers
  • Further postgraduate research or education

Entry Requirements

An undergraduate degree (with research methods and minimum Overall A- Grade) in any discipline and 3 years of experience OR a Masters' Degree in any discipline with 1 year's experience.

Assessment

Comprises a combination of coursework, in-class tests, and a major research project (30,000 words).
