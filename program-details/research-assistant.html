Certificate in Research Assistantship

Enhanced skills training for research assistants to support principal investigators and research teams effectively.

Duration
3-6 Months
Format
On-campus / Virtual
Fees

Program Overview

This innovative programme aims to provide existing or aspiring research assistants with enhanced skills to support the work of principal investigators and their research teams.

Currently, many graduates and non-graduates enter the field of research assistantship but have to build practical skills on the job. While this experiential learning is critical, the new recruits take time to acquire all the critical competencies. Thus, research teams may lose valuable time in training and basic skills development.

This programme serves as pre-service training and in-service staff development, equipping participants with essential competencies for effective research support.

Programme Modules

  • Role of the research assistant in the research team
  • Qualitative data collection
  • Quantitative data collection
  • Experiments
  • Research Ethics
  • Data Storage and administration

Programme Structure

The programme can be completed in as little as 1 term (3 months) if students take two modules a month, or in 2 terms (6 months) taking approximately one module a month.

As the programme is modular, the student and their research team may elect to do only selected modules that suit their needs.

Assessment

  • Coursework
  • In-class test
  • A simulated research assistant job interview as a capstone

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this programme are prepared for roles as:

  • Research Assistants in academic institutions
  • Research Support Staff in private sector
  • Laboratory Assistants
  • Data Collection Specialists
  • Research Coordinators

Entry Requirements

A college certificate or diploma. Some research experience will be an added advantage.
