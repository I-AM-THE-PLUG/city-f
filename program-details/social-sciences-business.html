Diploma in Social Sciences and Business

Foundation programme preparing students for diverse roles in human services, administration, and private enterprise.

Duration
2 Years
Format

Fees

Program Overview

An initial diploma for those who have just completed their higher school grade. It prepares you for a variety of roles in human services, administration, or private enterprise. You will learn about individual and group behavior as well as how to work with people and deal with various workplace situations.

Have you just finished high school? Try our degree programme and develop your skills for the world of work. This program is for you if you have completed high school and want to continue your learning with a diploma to give you the skills needed for modern work.

Programme Curriculum

  • Psychology
  • The global world
  • Sociology
  • Introduction to business
  • Financial Management
  • Human resources management
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Employability skills
  • Computer skills
  • Project management
  • Internship (5000 words) – in business or in human services

Programme Features

This comprehensive programme combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, including:

  • Understanding of individual and group behavior
  • Practical workplace skills development
  • Real-world internship experience
  • Comprehensive business foundation
  • Professional skill development

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this programme are prepared for various roles including:

  • Administrators in the public or private sectors
  • Office workers and coordinators
  • Customer service representatives
  • Human services assistants
  • Business support staff
  • Continuation of professional or higher education

Entry Requirements

A high school diploma or work experience if you left high school without. This programme is generally aimed at young adults.

Assessment

Includes a combination of coursework, in-class tests, presentations, and a research project (internship report).
