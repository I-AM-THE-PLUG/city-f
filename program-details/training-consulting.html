Program Overview

This comprehensive certificate programme provides an advanced understanding of training and consulting practice with solid grounding in adult learning theories and practical skills. Designed for professionals seeking to enhance their capabilities in organizational development and learning facilitation.

The programme combines theoretical foundations with hands-on application, preparing participants to design, deliver, and evaluate effective training programmes while developing consulting skills for organizational improvement.

Programme Curriculum

  • Adult Learning Theories and Principles
  • Training Needs Analysis
  • Instructional Design and Curriculum Development
  • Training Delivery and Facilitation Skills
  • Consulting Models and Approaches
  • Organizational Development and Change Management
  • Evaluation and Assessment of Training Effectiveness
  • Ethics in Training and Consulting
  • Technology in Training and Development
  • Business Development for Consultants

Programme Features

This practical programme emphasizes real-world application and skill development:

  • Hands-on training design and delivery practice
  • Case studies from various industries
  • Expert instruction from experienced trainers and consultants
  • Peer learning and networking opportunities
  • Portfolio development for career advancement
  • Flexible scheduling for working professionals

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this programme are prepared for various roles including:

  • Corporate Trainer
  • Learning and Development Specialist
  • Organizational Development Consultant
  • Training Coordinator
  • Independent Consultant
  • HR Development Professional
  • Change Management Specialist
  • Performance Improvement Consultant

Entry Requirements

This programme is suitable for professionals with at least two years of work experience. A background in HR, education, management, or related fields is beneficial but not required. Strong communication skills and a commitment to professional development are essential.

Assessment

Includes practical assignments, training session delivery, case analysis, consulting project, and portfolio development.
