Program Overview

Put your research into practice when you visit us as a new scholar. We welcome graduates who have recently completed their doctorate and wish to come to C-IHL as visiting scholars for a short period to interact with our team of researchers to pursue research.

This programme is mainly for early career scholars who completed their doctoral research no more than 2 years at the time of their application. Note that at present you need your funding. Call or email us for more details.

As a postdoctoral visiting scholar at C-IHL, you will receive a comprehensive orientation that encapsulates cultural issues, the economy and politics of Canada, and finding your way around.

Programme Features

  • Allocated mentor for personalized guidance
  • Development of thesis or previous research into publishable papers
  • Submission support for academic publications
  • Opportunity to pursue new research topics with in-house expertise
  • Teaching experience in the postsecondary sector in Canada
  • Full integration into the Institute's academic life
  • Reflective report and certification upon completion

Length of Postdoctoral Visit

This can be 3, 6 or 12 months depending on the funding that you have.

  • 3 months: Receive a letter of reference on completion
  • 6 or 12 months: Receive formal certification with a transcript showing the activities you undertook and completed

Benefits

This program offers early career researchers the opportunity to:

  • Enhance their research portfolio with international experience
  • Gain teaching experience in Canadian higher education
  • Network with established researchers and academics
  • Receive mentorship for career development
  • Produce publishable research outputs

Entry Requirements

Completion of doctoral degree within the last 2 years. Applicants must secure their own funding for the duration of the visit.
