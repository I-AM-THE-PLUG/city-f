For applicants with managerial experience, focusing on global business management with significant research component.

Program Overview

The Higher Learning Diploma in International Management is designed for experienced managers seeking to enhance their global business capabilities. This advanced programme combines rigorous academic study with practical research components to develop strategic thinking in international business contexts.

Participants will explore complex global business environments, cross-cultural management challenges, and international strategic planning. The programme emphasizes research skills and analytical thinking to prepare managers for leadership roles in multinational organizations.

Programme Curriculum

  • Global Strategic Management
  • International Business Environment
  • Cross-Cultural Management and Leadership
  • Global Marketing Strategy
  • International Finance and Risk Management
  • Global Supply Chain and Operations
  • International Human Resource Management
  • Research Methods in International Business
  • Emerging Markets and Global Economics
  • International Business Law and Ethics

Research Component

This programme includes a significant research component where students undertake:

  • Comprehensive literature review in international management
  • Research proposal development
  • Primary or secondary data collection and analysis
  • 15,000-word research dissertation
  • Research presentation and defense

Programme Features

This advanced diploma offers unique features for experienced professionals:

  • Research-focused curriculum with academic rigor
  • Case studies from global business scenarios
  • International business simulations
  • Networking with global business professionals
  • Access to international business databases
  • Supervision by experienced international business faculty

Career Opportunities

Graduates are prepared for senior international roles including:

  • International Business Manager
  • Global Strategy Director
  • International Operations Manager
  • Cross-Cultural Consultant
  • International Marketing Manager
  • Global Business Development Manager
  • Multinational Corporation Executive
  • International Trade Specialist

Entry Requirements

Bachelor's degree and minimum 5 years of managerial experience. Demonstrated interest in international business. Strong analytical and research capabilities. International work experience preferred but not required.

Assessment

Includes research papers, case analyses, strategic business plans, comprehensive research dissertation, and oral defense.
