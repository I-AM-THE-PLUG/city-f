Develop executive expertise in modern managerial practice with specializations in Marketing, HR, Logistics, or Supply Chain Management.

Program Overview

The Executive Diploma in Management (EDM) is designed for professionals seeking to develop executive expertise in modern managerial practice. This comprehensive programme provides advanced knowledge and skills in core business functions while offering specialized pathways in high-demand areas.

Participants will gain strategic thinking capabilities, leadership competencies, and practical management skills essential for senior roles in today's dynamic business environment. The programme combines theoretical foundations with real-world applications to prepare executives for complex organizational challenges.

Core Curriculum

  • Strategic Management and Leadership
  • Financial Management for Executives
  • Organizational Behavior and Development
  • Operations and Supply Chain Management
  • Marketing Strategy and Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Business Analytics and Decision Making
  • Corporate Governance and Ethics
  • Innovation and Change Management
  • Executive Communication and Negotiation

Specialization Tracks

Marketing Management

Focus on advanced marketing strategies, digital marketing, brand management, and customer relationship management.

  • Digital Marketing Strategy
  • Brand Management and Development
  • Consumer Behavior Analysis
  • Marketing Analytics and ROI

Human Resource Management

Develop expertise in talent management, organizational development, compensation strategies, and HR analytics.

  • Strategic HR Management
  • Talent Acquisition and Development
  • Performance Management Systems
  • Employment Law and Compliance

Logistics and Supply Chain Management

Master supply chain optimization, logistics management, procurement strategies, and global operations.

  • Supply Chain Strategy and Design
  • Logistics and Distribution Management
  • Procurement and Supplier Management
  • Global Supply Chain Operations

Programme Features

This executive programme offers distinctive features for professional development:

  • Case-based learning from real business scenarios
  • Executive coaching and mentorship
  • Networking with senior professionals
  • Flexible scheduling for working executives
  • Industry projects and consulting opportunities
  • Access to executive development resources

Career Opportunities

Graduates are prepared for senior management roles including:

  • Senior Manager/Director
  • Department Head
  • Business Unit Leader
  • Operations Manager
  • Marketing Director
  • HR Manager
  • Supply Chain Manager
  • General Manager

Entry Requirements

Bachelor's degree or equivalent professional experience. Minimum 3 years of managerial or supervisory experience preferred. Strong leadership potential and commitment to professional growth.

Assessment

Includes case studies, strategic projects, executive presentations, and comprehensive business plan development.
