Specialist Diploma by Research

A research-based programme for professionals seeking to undertake independent research inquiry under expert supervision.

Program Overview

This programme primarily based on research aims to equip learners to carry out a supervised piece of research leading to a Specialist Diploma by Research. The programme is for professionals and graduates who did not undertake a research-based dissertation or project during their academic or professional training and would now like to embark on an independent research inquiry.

Programme Levels

Level 4 Diploma (Undergraduate): For those with an undergraduate education. Requires completion and defense of a 10,000-word research project supervised by one of our qualified lecturers or researchers.

Level 5 Diploma (Postgraduate): For candidates who have done some postgraduate studies. Requires completion and defense of a more advanced 12,000-15,000-word project under the supervision of one of our qualified lecturers or researchers.

Note: For those planning to do a project in science, please contact us to discuss your area in the first instance as we have only limited laboratory arrangements.

Programme Curriculum

  • Research Methods
  • Project report
  • Defense of the project report

Career Opportunities

Graduates of this programme can expect:

  • Progression in the candidates' field of work
  • Enhanced research capabilities for professional advancement
  • Preparation for further studies
  • Improved analytical and critical thinking skills

Entry Requirements

Level 4: College diploma or an undergraduate degree

Level 5: Postgraduate diploma

Assessment

Project report/dissertation and a viva presentation (oral defense).
