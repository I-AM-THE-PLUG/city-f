Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to a program?

Visit our Programs page to explore available programs, then contact our admissions team at admin@city-ihl.ca for application details and requirements.

Do you offer virtual appointments?

Yes, we offer virtual meetings via video conference for all departments. Contact us to schedule a virtual appointment.

What are the language options for communication?

We communicate in both English and French. Select your preferred language from the language selector at the top of the page.

How long does it take to receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Urgent matters are prioritized.
